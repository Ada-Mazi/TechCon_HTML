Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 200 technology enthusiasts. Over the years, we've grown into one of the premier tech conferences globally, hosting over 10,000 attendees annually.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and knowledge sharing within the global technology community. We believe in the power of technology to solve complex problems and improve lives.
